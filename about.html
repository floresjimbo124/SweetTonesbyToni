About Sweets by Toni

Freshly baked, joyfully designed. Learn the story behind the sweets.

I’m Toni — a self-taught baker crafting custom cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and pastries in dreamy pastels. Every order is baked fresh with premium ingredients and lots of love.

What started as weekend bakes for friends has grown into a small bakeshop focused on celebrations: birthdays, weddings, and every sweet moment in between. My style mixes playful color with clean, modern detailing — from ombré buttercream to sprinkle-kissed borders.

Have a vision in mind? I love working on custom orders. Share your favorite flavors and inspiration photos, and we’ll design something magical together.

Lead time: 5–7 days for standard cakes; 10–14 days for custom designs.
